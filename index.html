User Experience Design

How I Redesigned ABC Enterprises Website for
a Better User Experience? A Case Study.

In this case study, we delve into the world of user experience (UX) design, focusing on the practical improvements made at ABC Enterprises, a mid-sized sustainable home decor website. Our goal is to provide a detailed, straightforward examination of the UX enhancements, challenges faced, and lessons learned throughout the design process.

Introduction

In today's digital landscape, the quality of user experience plays a pivotal role in the success of any online platform. This case study takes a close look at how we've enhanced the user experience on ABC Enterprises, an e-commerce site dedicated to sustainable home decor.

  • The ABC Enterprises Context:

    ABC Enterprises is more than just an online store; it's a platform committed to promoting sustainability in home decor. We'll explore how we've navigated the specific challenges and opportunities of a mid-sized website with a focus on eco-friendly products.

  • Approach:

    I'll outline our step-by-step approach to improving the user experience on ABC Enterprises. From the initial research and analysis to the practical phases of sitemapping and wireframing, I'll share the strategies and methodologies that guided the design decisions.

  • User-Centric Design:

    At the heart of the case study is the principle of user-centric design. I'll delve into the creation of user personas, user journeys, and usability testing, illustrating how the design choices were grounded in a deep understanding of the user's needs and preferences.
